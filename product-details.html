Convex table

The Shift+ ThumbPrint is a stackable 4-legged convex table. When paired with the coordinating Shift+ ThumbPrint concave table, it can be arranged into endless configurations – circles, ovals, or freely shaped groups – creating truly agile designs.
  • Frame made from welded steel with round steel legs
  • Available in 7 fixed heights or step height adjustable with hexagon screw fastening, heights in accordance with DIN EN 1729
  • Stackable up to 6, with stacking protection on the underside of the frame
  • 2 glides and 2 lockable casters or glides for hard floors, soft floors, or 2-component universal glides
  • Available with a writable tabletop surface
